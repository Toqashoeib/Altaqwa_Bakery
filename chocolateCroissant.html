chocolate Croissants

Ingredients:

  • precise
  • butter
  • sugar
  • milk
  • yeast
  • salt
  • black chocolate

Directions

In a small bowl, mix the yeast with the hot milk and set aside.
In a large bowl, mix the flour, sugar and salt, then add the milk mixed with the yeast and knead well.
Spread the dough on a floured surface, then put the pieces of butter on it and fold the dough three times.
Put the dough into the fridge for about 15 minutes.
Take the dough out of the fridge, roll it out again, fold it again three times, and put it in the fridge for an additional 15 minutes.
Roll out the dough to a thickness of one centimeter and cut it into rectangles of the size you want.
Place chocolate pieces in the middle of each rectangle, then fold the ends of the rectangle towards the middle. Repeat until all the dough is finished.
Place the croissants on a tray lined with parchment paper and let them rest at room temperature for about an hour, or until they double in size.
heat the oven to 180 degree Celsius.
Enter the tray into the oven for 20 minutes.
Serve the chocolate croissants warm or cold with tea or coffee.
