chocolate cookies

Ingredients:

  • precise
  • chocolate
  • butter
  • sugar
  • egg
  • Baking soda
  • Vanillia
  • salt

Directions

1. The oven is heated to a high temperature.
2. Beat the butter and sugar, then put the vanilla, then the eggs, and mix well.
3. Mix the flour in another bowl, then put the baking soda and salt together, then pour over the egg mixture.
4. Add the chocolate granules and mix well.
5. Divide the dough into small balls, then place them in the oven tray.
6. Bake in the oven for 11 minutes.
