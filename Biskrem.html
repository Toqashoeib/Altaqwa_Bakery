Biskrem

Ingredients:

  • precise
  • Sugar Icing
  • Starch
  • butter
  • baking powder
  • Raw cocoa
  • vanilla

Directions

In a large bowl, mix together the butter, vanilla, and all the dry ingredients.
Add the rest of the ingredients except cocoa.
Divide the dough and put cocoa in half of it and the rest without.
Roll the dough into balls, alternating by hand.
Shape the dough in half.
Put the biscuits in the oven until they are done.
