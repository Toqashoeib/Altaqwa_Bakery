Bread

Ingredients:

  • precise
  • sugar
  • warm water
  • salt
  • yeast
  • oil

Directions

Mix flour, salt, baking powder, then oil.
Rub the flour until it is completely mixed with the rest of the ingredients.
Add water until you have a soft, non-sticky dough.
Leave the dough to rest for half an hour in the fridge.
Cut it into equal pieces, then put the sesame.
Put the baton in the oven until its face turns red.
