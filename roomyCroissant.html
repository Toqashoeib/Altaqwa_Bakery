roomy Croissants

Ingredients:

  • Puff pastry dough
  • Grated Romano cheese
  • Olive slices
  • sesame

Directions

Preheat the oven at 180 degrees Celsius.
Spread the puff pastry dough on a dry surface.
Cut the dough in the form of large triangles.
Divide the Romano cheese into the dough triangles, then place the olive slices evenly on top.
Roll the dough triangles over the filling, starting from the large part (the base of the dough triangle) to the other end.
Put the croissants on a tray that can be used in the oven, then put the sesame seeds on top.
Put the tray in the oven for 12-15 minutes.
Serve with tea as a breakfast meal.
